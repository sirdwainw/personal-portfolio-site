About Me

I am a dedicated technology professional with a strong background in software development, systems analysis, and data integration. Over the years, I’ve built expertise in Python, Java, C++, C#, SQL, and API integrations, and I’ve applied these skills in roles that span both technical and support functions. My career began with positions at Amazon as an IT Support Technician and later at Master Meter as a Systems Specialist and Systems Analyst/Trainer, where I focused on optimizing processes and supporting smart utility solutions. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Colorado Technical University, and I am now considering pursuing a doctorate to continue expanding my knowledge and leadership in the field.

I’ve had the opportunity to lead and contribute to projects that bring together technical innovation and real-world application. Some of my most exciting work includes developing customer-facing portals for water utilities and building creative applications like Presto, a mobile app designed for meter data management, and Urban Doodle, a word game powered by the Urban Dictionary API. My focus has always been on usability and scalability—designing solutions that not only function effectively but also improve the experience for the end user.

Beyond my technical journey, I take pride in balancing my professional growth with my personal life. As a full-time professional, graduate, husband, and father, I stay motivated by the opportunity to set an example of resilience, adaptability, and continuous learning. Looking ahead, I’m committed to expanding my expertise, mentoring others, and possibly pursuing doctoral research to further my impact in the technology space.
